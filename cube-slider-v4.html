Eira do Serrado

Eira do Serrado is a stunning viewpoint overlooking the picturesque Nuns’ Valley (Curral das Freiras) on the Portuguese island of Madeira. From this vantage point at an elevation of 1095 meters, you are treated to breathtaking panoramic vistas of the lush valley nestled between dramatic, near-vertical mountains. Embarking on a sensory journey to the heart of Madeira’s stunning landscapes.
